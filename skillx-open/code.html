Project Summary

The Online Mock Test Platform is a web-based application designed to help students prepare effectively for placements and job interviews. This platform provides a simulated exam environment where students can take practice tests to assess their knowledge, skills, and readiness for real-world interviews. It offers a variety of mock tests covering different subjects, topics, and difficulty levels, enabling students to identify their strengths and areas for improvement. The platform includes timed assessments, detailed result analysis, and performance tracking to help students build confidence and refine their strategies. The goal is to make students well-prepared and self-assured for their upcoming placements and interviews.
